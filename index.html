Frequently Asked Questions

What is KHDA approval? ▼
KHDA (Knowledge and Human Development Authority) approval is the official permit required to operate any educational or training institution in Dubai. It ensures your institute meets Dubai's quality education standards.
Who needs KHDA approval in Dubai? ▼
Any training center, nursery, tutoring institute, online academy, school, or university offering educational services within Dubai must obtain KHDA approval before operating.
How long does the KHDA approval process take? ▼
Typically 2-7 months depending on the complexity of your institution. With Aspira's expert guidance, most clients receive approval within 30-60 days.
How much does KHDA approval cost? ▼
KHDA fees range from AED 10,000 for nurseries to AED 50,000 for private schools. Total setup costs (including licensing, documentation, and consultancy) typically range from AED 30,000 to AED 200,000+ depending on institution type.
Can I operate without KHDA approval? ▼
No. Operating without KHDA approval is illegal and can result in fines up to AED 100,000 and forced closure of your institution.
What documents are needed for KHDA approval? ▼
Trade license, tenancy contract, Civil Defense certificate, academic & business plan, staff qualifications/attested degrees, passport copies, and KHDA portal application. We handle all documentation for you.
