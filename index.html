FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about KHDA approval in Dubai.

What is KHDA approval? ▼
KHDA (Knowledge and Human Development Authority) approval is the official permit required to operate any educational or training institution in Dubai. It ensures your institute meets Dubai's quality education standards and is legally authorized to operate.
Who needs KHDA approval in Dubai? ▼
Any training center, nursery, tutoring institute, online academy, private school, arts academy, music school, sports academy, or university offering educational services within Dubai must obtain KHDA approval before beginning operations.
How long does the KHDA approval process take? ▼
The process typically takes 2–7 months depending on institution complexity. With Aspira's expert guidance and preparation, most clients receive approval within 30–60 days. Our fastest case study was approved in 30 days.
How much does KHDA approval cost? ▼
KHDA government fees range from AED 10,000 (nurseries) to AED 20,000 (non-profit) to AED 50,000 (private schools). Total setup costs including licensing, documentation, and consultancy range from AED 30,000 to AED 200,000+ depending on institution type and scope.
Can I operate without KHDA approval? ▼
No. Operating without KHDA approval is illegal in Dubai. You risk fines up to AED 100,000, forced closure of your institution, and legal action. All educational activities — including online and corporate training — require KHDA authorization.
What documents are needed for KHDA approval? ▼
You need a trade license, tenancy contract (Ejari), Civil Defense certificate, academic & business plan, staff qualifications with attested degrees, passport copies, NOCs (if applicable), and marketing materials. We handle every document for you.
Should I choose mainland or free zone for my education business? ▼
Mainland licenses offer flexibility to operate anywhere in Dubai and serve local clients directly. Free zones (Dubai Knowledge Park, Dubai Academic City) provide ready education infrastructure and streamlined KHDA coordination. We help you choose based on your specific business model.
How do I renew my KHDA approval? ▼
KHDA approval must be renewed annually. The process involves updating documents, confirming staff credentials, and ensuring continued facility compliance. We manage your renewal end-to-end to avoid any lapse in your license.
