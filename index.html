FAQ

Common questions about KHDA approval

Straight answers to the questions we hear every day.

What is KHDA approval? ↓
KHDA (Knowledge and Human Development Authority) approval is the official permit required to operate any educational or training institution in Dubai. It means your institute meets the emirate's quality standards and is legally authorised to operate.
Who needs KHDA approval? ↓
Any training centre, nursery, tutoring institute, online academy, private school, arts academy, music school, sports academy or university operating in Dubai must obtain KHDA approval before beginning operations.
How long does the process take? ↓
Typically 2–7 months depending on complexity. With our preparation, most clients receive approval within 30–60 days. Our fastest case study was approved in 30 days.
How much does KHDA approval cost? ↓
Government fees range from AED 10,000 (nurseries) to AED 20,000 (non-profit training centres) to AED 50,000 (private schools). Total setup costs including licensing, documentation and consultancy typically range from AED 30,000 to AED 200,000+.
Can I operate without KHDA approval? ↓
No. Operating without KHDA approval is illegal. Penalties include fines up to AED 100,000, forced closure and legal action. This applies to all educational activities including online and corporate training.
What documents are needed? ↓
Trade license, tenancy contract (Ejari), Civil Defence certificate, academic and business plan, staff qualifications with attested degrees, passport copies, NOCs and marketing materials. We handle all of these.
Mainland or free zone — which is better? ↓
Mainland gives you location flexibility and direct market access. Free zones offer ready education infrastructure and faster KHDA coordination. Your choice depends on your target students, budget and expansion plans.
How do I renew my KHDA license? ↓
KHDA approval must be renewed annually. The process involves updating documents, confirming staff credentials and verifying facility compliance. We manage the full renewal cycle so your license never lapses.
